Cover design for fictional magazine Oliver


Oliver is a magazine touching upon contemporary issues; politics, culture and society. In correlation with the vide scope of contents on the pages, the covers varies greatly in expression. With a fixed position in the top of the cover, the logo is presented as a narrow banner, along with the headlines in the same banner style. A simple yet unique text look which becomes the identity of the magazine.
