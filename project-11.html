Cultural expressions and the inherent ambiguity and flexibility of language pose risks for communication-gaps and meanings lost in translation.


This book documents the extent of errors and mistranslations occurring when translating 3000 English words -> to Danish -> and back to English again with Google Translate, and explores what happens in that space of algorithmic translation.
